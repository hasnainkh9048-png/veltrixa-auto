How to Buy a Vehicle from Veltrixa Auto

Simple 4-step process to get your dream Japanese vehicle

1

Browse & Select

Browse our extensive inventory of Japanese vehicles. Use our search filters to find the perfect vehicle that matches your requirements - by make, model, year, price, and more.

2

Contact Us

Once you've found a vehicle you're interested in, contact us via WhatsApp or Email. We'll provide you with detailed information, including:

  • Full vehicle history and condition report
  • High-resolution photos and videos
  • Total cost including shipping to your country
  • Estimated delivery time

3

Payment & Documentation

After confirming your order, we'll guide you through the payment process. We accept bank transfers and other secure payment methods. We'll handle all export documentation from Japan.

4

Shipping & Delivery

We arrange international shipping to your nearest port. Our shipping partners ensure safe and timely delivery. We'll provide you with tracking information and assist with customs clearance documentation.
